Our Story

More Than a Restaurant —
It's Family.

Born from New Mexico roots, built in Southern California, and running on love since 2013.

Tony and Lina Esqueda brought the authentic flavors of Tucumcari, New Mexico to La Habra over a decade ago. With more than 30 years of cooking tradition behind them, The Green Chile is a family restaurant in the truest sense — Tony in the kitchen every day, Lina greeting guests by name, and their children carrying on the tradition. Every dish is made fresh, every day, from recipes that have been in the family for generations.

Authentic Recipes — Rooted in New Mexico heritage & family tradition.
Made Fresh Daily — Everything cooked to order, never frozen or reheated.
Family Run — The Esqueda family is here every day, personally serving you.
Community Focused — Proud to serve La Habra & the surrounding community.
